Avengers:Endgame

In "Avengers: Endgame," the Avengers are devastated after Thanos wipes out half of all life in the universe with the Infinity Stones. They devise a plan to travel back in time using the Quantum Realm to collect the Stones from the past and undo Thanos' actions. This leads to epic battles, emotional reunions, and a final showdown against Thanos, resulting in the restoration of the snapped individuals and the conclusion of the Infinity Saga in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
